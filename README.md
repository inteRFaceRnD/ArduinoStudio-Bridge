# ArduinoStudio Bridge

The Bridge is a lightweight desktop app that connects your browser to your Arduino board. It runs silently in your system tray and lets [ArduinoStudio](https://app.arduinostudio.com) communicate with your hardware in real time.

## Download

**[Download the latest version](https://www.arduinostudio.com/download)**

| Platform | Requirements |
|----------|-------------|
| Windows  | Windows 10+ (64-bit) |
| macOS    | macOS 12+ (Apple Silicon & Intel) |
| Linux    | Ubuntu/Debian (.deb) |

## How it works

1. **Install the Bridge** on your computer
2. **Open ArduinoStudio** in your browser at [app.arduinostudio.com](https://app.arduinostudio.com)
3. **Plug in your Arduino** — the Bridge detects it automatically
4. **Click Play** — your sequence runs on real hardware

The Bridge communicates with your Arduino using the [Firmata](https://github.com/firmata/protocol) protocol. ArduinoStudio handles the setup for you — just click "Set Up Board" in the wizard.

## Windows SmartScreen

Windows may show a blue SmartScreen warning when you first run the installer. This is normal for new apps and does **not** mean the app is dangerous.

1. Click **"More info"**
2. Click **"Run anyway"**
